How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe:

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe! Take turns placing your mark ("X" or "O") on the 3x3 grid. The first player to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins! Click on an empty cell to make your move. The game ends when a player wins or the board is filled, resulting in a tie. To start a new game, refresh the page. Have fun playing this classic game of strategy!
